wave to earth (웨이브투어스) is a 3-member band under Wavy, consisting of Daniel Kim, Soonjong Cha, and Dong Q. Originally the band consisted of Shin Donggyu and Daniel, debuting with their first single, wave on August 23, 2019. In January of 2020, Wave To Earth released their first EP [Wave 0.01] with their new member Soonjong Cha. While the three of them are the official members, the session members also include Jo Jung-geun (keyboard), Jeon-min (saxophone), and Hong Seung-gi, an artwork member.

Daniel Kim (Lead Vocalist)

Daniel Kim is the charismatic lead vocalist of Wave to Earth, known for his dynamic stage presence and emotive voice. With a passion for music that spans multiple genres, Daniel brings a unique blend of energy and sincerity to every performance. His dedication to his craft and ability to connect with audiences through heartfelt lyrics make him the heart of the band. When he's not on stage, Daniel enjoys exploring new music and drawing inspiration from various artists around the world.

Soonjong Cha (Guitarist)

Soonjong Cha is the talented guitarist whose intricate riffs and melodic solos shape the band's distinctive sound. His versatility allows Wave to Earth to explore a wide range of musical styles, from rock to pop to indie. With a deep understanding of music theory and a knack for creating powerful arrangements, Soonjong's contributions are central to the band's sonic identity. Outside of music, he’s known for his creativity and love of experimenting with new guitar techniques and sounds.

Dong Q (Drummer)

Dong Q is the powerhouse drummer of Wave to Earth, providing the solid foundation that drives the band's rhythm section. His impeccable timing, precision, and energetic performances add an unmistakable groove to every track. Dong's passion for percussion shines through in every performance, making him an essential part of the band's live shows. In his free time, Dong enjoys exploring different drumming styles and collaborating with other musicians to push the boundaries of rhythm.
